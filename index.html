Balerion

Balerion, called the Black Dread, was a dragon of House Targaryen. He was ridden by King Aegon I Targaryen during the War of Conquest.

Syrax

Syrax was a female dragon. She was exclusively ridden by Rhaenyra Targaryen. Her name came from a Valyrian goddess.

Arrax

Arrax was a dragon ridden by Prince Lucerys Velaryon during the Dance of the Dragons.

Caraxes

Caraxes, also called the Blood Wyrm and Bloodworm, was the dragon ridden by Prince Aemon Targaryen during the reign of King Jaehaerys I Targaryen and later by Prince Daemon Targaryen.

Seasmoke

Seasmoke was a light gray dragon. He was large enough for combat during the Dance of the Dragons, but still a young dragon, and more agile in the air than his older siblings.

Vermax

Vermax was the dragon ridden by Prince Jacaerys Velaryon. He thrived and grew each year. The dragon became irritable in the presence of snow, ice, and cold. Vermax's color is not described in the books.

Vhagar

Vhagar was a female dragon ridden by Visenya during the Conquest, alongside Aegon the Conqueror's Balerion and Meraxes, ridden by his sister Rhaenys.
